About

J de Pala is a PNW transplant originally from the San Francisco Bay Area and has lived in Seattle since 2017. His time is split between attending school for his Doctorate of Physical Therapy, taking photos, and playing eSports with his friends.

J shoots both digital and film photography and enjoys capturing day to day scenes of the city. His current favorit camera is his Olympus XA.

Photography Resources:

Local Seattle:
  • Glazers - was founded in 1935 and offers a great selection of new and used equipment for purchase or for rent, a broad selection of film stock, and a friendly and accomodating staff.
  • Panda Lab - opened in 1985 and is a great spot to have your film developed, scanned, or printed.
  • Shot on Film Store - is located just north of Seattle in Lake City and both buys and sells equipment as well as develops and scans film.
