About

J de Pala is a PNW transplant originally from the San Francisco Bay Area and has lived in Seattle since 2017. His time is split between attending school for his Doctorate of Physical Therapy, taking photos, and playing eSports with his friends.

De Pala shoots both digital and film photography and enjoys capturing day to day scenes of the city. If he had to pick one camera to bring to a desert island (with an infinite film supply and an inexplicable way to develop it), he'd choose his Olympus XA.
